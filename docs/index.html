Never miss EuroBonus points again.

EB Finder is a Safari Web Extension for iPhone and iPad that spots SAS EuroBonus shopping partners and nudges you to log in via SAS's tracking link before you buy — so your purchase actually earns points.

Why it exists

SAS ships an official EB Finder for Chrome that nudges you when a site is a EuroBonus partner — but it's desktop Chrome only. Nothing for iPhone or iPad, where most of the everyday shopping happens. So this fills the gap.

Highlights

  • Works where the official one doesn't

    Safari on iPhone and iPad — the platforms the official Chrome extension skips.

  • Same data as the official extension

    Hits the same public LoyaltyKey API, so the partner list and points-per-krona stay in sync automatically.

  • Zero tracking

    No analytics, no background script. No data leaves your device beyond the two API calls.

How it works

  1. Join the beta via TestFlight and install the app on your iPhone or iPad.
  2. Enable the extension in Settings → Apps → Safari → Extensions, and allow it on all websites.
  3. Shop as usual. When a site is a EuroBonus partner, EB Finder nudges you to log in via SAS first — then your purchase earns points.
